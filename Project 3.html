MIAU

This project's main goal was to create a story-based game where the main character was not human.

Project Info

Role: Tyler James Ponce

Course: IDEA120

Tools: HTML, CSS, Twine

Overview

Because of my love for cats, I chose to create a game from the point of view of a cat living in a city called New Bark City. The city is shaped by species-based conflict, and the player can follow several routes that lead to both good and bad endings.
